Who We are

Welcome to Utawala

Politician & Founder

Nobody can teach me who I am. You can describe parts of me, but who I am - and what I need - is something I have to find out myself. ~Chinua Achebe

I am currently serving as Utawala MCA and founder Utawala Talent Academy. I am also serving in finance committee in Nairobi County Assembly. I offer Utawala residents, better infrastructure, youth empowerment, access to education among an endless list of my mandate as elected. Also not forgetting, I have ensured land reforms, and overseeing the funds allocated are spent to benefit the ordinary mwananchi. I am transformational and look foward to making Utawala great.

Issues & Plans

Due to the covid-19 pandemic and economic recession, most Utawalans lived paycheck to paycheck also noting that the life expectancy has also decreased as a result.

In order to make that more equitable future a reality, Hon. Karani has empowered more youths in Utawala, has made it possible under his leadership to improved infrastructure, this in turn expands the social security, for everyone.

"We have constructed roads in Fedha Estate”

On the final laps of completing the reconstruction of Flamingo Avenue Road, Fedha Estate. With the grace and determination of these young men.

SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION

We have went ahead and constructed more schools, to accomodate the increase in student enrollment around Utawala. More classes are being built.

PROVISION OF WATER

Through my capable team, access to water is now available through piping, and we are still seeing to it that no water cartels, who make water scarse anymore.

ECONOMIC OPPORTUNITY

We have empowered the youth with bodabodas, also supported them in various ventures that have started and earning them an income.

HEALTH CARE

Under my administration, there's has been enhanced health care for all Utawalans and access is more available, for everyone. Health is wealth.
